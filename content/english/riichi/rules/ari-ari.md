**Ari-ari** {{< kana "アリ アリ" >}} is generally the most commonly accepted ruleset pertaining to
Japanese mahjong. The two instances of "ari" (or "on") applies to the rules of kuitan and atozuke.

## Game settings

- Agariyame - Nashi
- Atamahane - Nashi
- Atozuke - Ari
- Kuikae - Nashi
- Kuitan - Ari
- Tobi - Ari

### Dora

- Akadora - Variable
- Kandora
- Uradora - Ari
