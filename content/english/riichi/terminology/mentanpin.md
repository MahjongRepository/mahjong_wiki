**Mentanpin** {{< kana "メンタンピン" >}} is an abbreviation for the combination of the following
three yaku:

- [Riichi]({{< ref "/riichi/rules/riichi.md" >}})
- [Tanyao]({{< ref "/riichi/yaku/tanyao.md" >}})
- [Pinfu]({{< ref "/riichi/yaku/pinfu.md" >}})
